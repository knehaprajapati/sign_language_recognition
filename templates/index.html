HUMAN ACTIVITY RECOGNITION



MINOR PROJECT

The primary goal of your project is to develop a real-time system that accurately recognizes sign language gestures using standard devices like webcams or smartphone cameras. This system aims to facilitate seamless communication between individuals who use sign language and those who do not, without the need for specialized hardware. Healthcare: Facilitate communication between medical professionals and patients who use sign language, improving the quality of care and patient experience.
Customer Service: Enhance interactions in retail, hospitality, and other service industries by enabling staff to understand and respond to sign language in real time.
Public Services: Improve accessibility in government offices, transportation systems, and public events by providing real-time sign language translation.
Personal Communication: Empower individuals to communicate more effectively with friends, family, and colleagues who may not understand sign language.
